EVENT DETAILS

This day brings together two events, a day of fun and laugther for autistic children and their families and a highly competitive Bass tournament. All designed to raise autism awareness and money for autism research!

August 17, 2015
Mystic Wellington Yacht Club

SUNDAY, AUGUST 17th - 10AM to 4PM
A Day of Fun, FIshing and Boating
$30 Donation Fee | Kids Come for FREE!
BBQ, fun & games, boat trips for kids,
bounce house, prizes & giveaways!

Adult Bass Tournament

TEAM or INDIVIDUAL 5 Fish Limit $150 Per Boat | 7:00AM - 3:00PM Includes BBQ, refreshments and all activities Guaranteed $1500 or more 1st Place! $500 or more for Big Fish! Please register and check in for tournament by 6:15 AM.
